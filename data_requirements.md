# DATA REQUIREMENTS

## 1. AUDIENCE DATA (من Analytics → Audience)

### 1.1 Other Channels Your Audience Watches (مهم جداً!)
Export or screenshot:
- Channel names
- Percentage/ranking

This tells us: WHO are our personas based on what else they watch

### 1.2 Other Videos Your Audience Watches (مهم جداً!)
Export or screenshot:
- Video titles
- Channel names
- Topics

This tells us: WHAT topics interest our audience right now

### 1.3 Demographics
- Countries with percentages
- Age groups with percentages  
- Gender split
- Device types

### 1.4 When Viewers Are Online
- Days of week
- Hours of day (by timezone)

---

## 2. VIDEO PERFORMANCE DATA (من Analytics → Content)

### 2.1 All Videos Export (CSV)
Fields needed:
- Video ID
- Title
- Publish Date
- Views
- Watch Time (hours)
- Average View Duration
- CTR (Click-through rate)
- Impressions
- Average Percentage Viewed
- Subscribers gained
- Likes
- Comments count

### 2.2 Traffic Sources
For top 20 videos:
- Browse features %
- Suggested videos %
- Search %
- External %
- Direct %

### 2.3 Search Terms
- Top 50 search terms
- Views from each term
- CTR from search

---

## 3. VIDEO DETAILS (من YouTube Studio → Content)

### 3.1 For Each Video:
- Full title
- Description (first 500 chars)
- Tags (all)
- Thumbnail (URL or file)
- Video length (seconds)
- Category
- Language

---

## 4. COMMENTS DATA

### 4.1 Recent Comments Export
- Last 500-1000 comments
- Or from top 20 videos

Fields:
- Comment text
- Video ID
- Likes on comment
- Is reply?
- Date

---

## 5. COMPETITOR DATA

### 5.1 Direct Competitors
For each competitor channel:
- Channel URL/ID
- Last 20 video titles
- View counts (if visible)
- Upload frequency

### 5.2 Adjacent Channels
(Channels your audience also watches)
- Channel URL/ID
- Channel category/niche
- Why audience watches them

---

## EXPORT FORMATS

Preferred: JSON or CSV

File naming:
- `audience_other_channels.json`
- `audience_other_videos.json`
- `audience_demographics.json`
- `video_performance.csv`
- `traffic_sources.json`
- `search_terms.csv`
- `video_details.csv`
- `comments.csv`
- `competitors.json`
